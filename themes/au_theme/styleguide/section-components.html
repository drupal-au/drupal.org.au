5 #components Components

Design components are reusable designs that can be applied using just the CSS class names specified in the component.

5.1 #components.article Article

Styling for topical content

Example

Article title

Date

Summary

<article class="article">
  <h1 class="article__title">Article title</h1>
  <p class="article__date">Date</p>
  <p class="article__summary">Summary</p>
</article>

Example

Article title

Summary

<article class="article--teaser">

  <p class="article--teaser__date">Date</p>
  <p class="article--teaser__summary">Summary</p>
